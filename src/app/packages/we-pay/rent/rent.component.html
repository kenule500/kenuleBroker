RENT

We understand that majority of Nigerians live in rented apartments and paying of rent sometimes can be a huge burden considering the high cost of housing in urban areas and other economic burdens, hence, the need for a special package that enables Nigerians live completely worry free over the issue of rent was birthed.

Description
  • Invest the total cost of your rent with KENULE Trading and Capital Investment Ten (10) months before payment is due.
  • KENULE Trading and Capital Investment pays you the exact amount yearly for the next three (3) years.
  • Minimum investment rate is Three Hundred Thousand Naira only (NGN300,000).
  • No third party involvement (No dealing with Landlord).
  • No Return on Invested capital.
